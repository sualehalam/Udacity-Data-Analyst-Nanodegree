Introduction¶

The purpose of this project is to practice the skills of Data Wrangling which I learned from Udacity's Data Analysis Nanodegree program. The dataset which we are working on is the tweet archive of @dog_rates, also known as WeRateDogs. This Twitter account rates dogs with humorous commentary, where the rating denominator is usually 10. However, the numerators are usually greater than 10. Some interesting visualizations will also be made at the end of this project.
